Davey Jones Locker

You've been given the name and location of a pub and the person you ought to talk to once in there. "The Davey Jones Locker" is what they call it, and you've heard of this place before. An Irish business where both drinks and boxing-matches are served in great amounts, for all those who enjoy good entertainment.

After taking a right turn into a small alleyway, you notice a big man almost as tall as the arcade above. You feel uneasy at first be he just stares intently as you pass. "Probably a security looking out for coppers." He did let you pass, and as you walk the sound of a subtle murmur of a crowd and an occasional laughter ahead start to echo inside the arcade. You finally arrive at a small courtyard, and there is a crowd alright. Mostly gangsters dressed up for a great evening ahead which was just starting early, probably another night of matches.
